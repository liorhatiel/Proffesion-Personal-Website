About Me

Hey there! I'm Lior Hatiel, a Front-End Developer with a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the Holon Institute of Technology (HIT). My journey in tech started during the Covid-19 pandemic, where I taught myself Unity game development. Since then, I’ve expanded my focus to front-end web development, gaining expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.

I’m passionate about crafting intuitive and responsive web applications that solve real-world problems. Through my hands-on projects, I’ve gained a deep understanding of both game and web development. My projects include interactive web games, financial tracking apps, and a personal portfolio to showcase my growth as a developer.

In addition to my technical skills, I actively showcase my projects on GitHub, where you can explore my work and contributions to open-source projects. I also value version control and collaborative work, utilizing Git to manage my projects efficiently.

I’m currently looking for job opportunities that will allow me to apply my expertise in front-end development and collaborate with a dynamic team. If you’re looking for a developer who combines creativity with strong technical skills, I’d love to connect!
