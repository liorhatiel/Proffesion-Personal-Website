About Me

Hey there! My name is Lior Hatiel. I'm an autodidact with a passion for technology and a knack for learning. My journey into the world of development began during the Covid-19 pandemic in 2020, well before I even kicked off my Computer Science degree. During those challenging times, I immersed myself in the intricacies of game development, driven by a self-taught spirit.

Fast forward to today, and I'm a Third-Year student pursuing a Computer Science degree at the Holon Institute of Technology (HIT). In addition to my academic journey, I've taken it upon myself to delve into the world of web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, despite it not being part of the official curriculum.

Balancing both my academic pursuits and hands-on experience, I've cultivated a unique blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ranging from game development to the dynamic realm of web technologies. I thrive on challenges and believe in the power of continuous learning. Every line of code I write is a testament to my dedication to mastering the art and science of computer science, encompassing both game and web development.

Excitingly, I'm open to job opportunities that allow me to apply and expand my skills. If you have a compelling opportunity that aligns with my passion and expertise, I'd love to hear about it!
